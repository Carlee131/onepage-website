Fact about puppies

By Carlee T.

Puppies are young dogs that are playful, curious, and love attention. They grow fast and learn a lot from playing with people and other dogs.




About puppies
  1. Puppies usually open their eyes around 2 weeks old.
  2. They learn social skills by playing with littermates.
  3. Most puppies start teething at 3-6 months.
  4. Puppies sleep a lot to help their growth.
  5. Training early helps them grow into well-behaved dogs.
